What Are The Fixed Features of Your Home?

Before diving into color options, consider your home's fixed features – elements you won't be altering, like brick, stone, roof shingles, and more. Categorize these features into cool-toned (with blue, green, or purple undertones) or warm-toned (featuring red, yellow, or orange undertones).

Knowing the overall tone guides you in selecting paint colors that harmonize with your fixed features. The beauty is that a vast range of colors offers both cool and warm variations, granting you numerous choices.
